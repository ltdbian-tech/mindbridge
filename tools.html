🎧 Quick Distraction Ideas

When thoughts feel overwhelming, shifting focus can help. Try one of these:

🎵 Play music

Put on a song you loved as a child, or something with a strong beat you can move to.

🚿 Cold water

Splash cold water on your face, hold an ice cube, or run cool water over your wrists.

📝 Write it out

Type or scribble everything you're feeling. Don't edit — just let it out on paper.

🐕 Move your body

Walk around the block, stretch, or do 20 jumping jacks. Physical movement shifts brain chemistry.

📞 Call someone

You don't have to talk about your feelings. Ask about their day, or watch a show together virtually.

🧩 Puzzle or game

Do a crossword, play a phone game, or sort something by color. Give your brain a different task.

💚 Self-Care Checklist

Small things matter. Check them off as you go — no pressure to do all of them.

  • Drank water in the last few hours
  • Eaten something nourishing today
  • Stepped outside or got fresh air
  • Slept at least a little in the past 24 hours
  • Spoken to another human today
  • Moved my body, even briefly
  • Taken a shower or changed clothes
  • Done one thing just for myself
