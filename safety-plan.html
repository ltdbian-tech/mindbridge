Your Personal Safety Plan

A safety plan helps you recognize warning signs, use coping strategies, and reach out before a crisis escalates. Fill this out when you're feeling okay — then keep it somewhere accessible.

Privacy note: This form never sends data anywhere. It stays only in your browser. You can print or save your plan when you're done.
