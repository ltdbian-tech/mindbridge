Delay the decision
by 15 minutes.

This is one of the most powerful techniques in crisis intervention. By waiting just 15 minutes, you give your brain time to shift. You give hope a chance to arrive. You are choosing to stay — and that matters.

15:00

You're doing something incredibly brave right now. Just by staying, you are choosing life.

Small things to do while you wait:

Click each one as you complete it. They add up.

✓
Take 5 slow breaths
✓
Drink a glass of water
✓

✓

✓

✓
Put on one song you love
✓
Hold something cold (ice, cold water)
✓
Name 3 things you can see right now

When the 15 minutes end...

You don't have to have solved everything. You just proved to yourself that you can wait. That you can survive the wave. If you still feel unsafe, please reach out to a crisis line. They are trained for this exact moment.
