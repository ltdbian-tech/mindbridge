If You're Not Sure You're in Crisis

Sometimes it's hard to tell. Here are signs that reaching out is a brave and good idea:

Thinking about hurting yourself

Even if you don't have a plan, these thoughts deserve attention and support.

Feeling hopeless about the future

If nothing seems like it will get better, talking to someone can help you see new possibilities.

Withdrawing from everyone

Isolation often makes things worse. Connection is a powerful antidote to pain.

Using substances to cope

If alcohol or drugs feel like your only escape, there are safer paths to relief.
