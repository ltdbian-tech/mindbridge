You don't have to go through this alone.

These services are free, confidential, and staffed by people trained to help. Reach out — even if you just need someone to listen.

🚨 If you are in immediate danger

Call your local emergency number

In most countries, dial 112 or 911. If you cannot call, try texting or using emergency features on your phone. Your life is worth saving.

Crisis Lines by Region

🇺🇸 United States — 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline

Free, confidential support for people in distress.

Call 988 24/7 · Free · Confidential

🇺🇸 United States — Crisis Text Line

Text-based crisis counseling with trained volunteers.

Text HOME to 741741 24/7 · Free · Confidential

🇬🇧 United Kingdom — Samaritans

Whatever you're going through, you can call Samaritans any time.

Call 116 123 24/7 · Free · Confidential

🇬🇧 United Kingdom — SHOUT

Free, confidential text support for anyone struggling to cope.

Text SHOUT to 85258 24/7 · Free · Confidential

🇨🇦 Canada — Talk Suicide Canada

Nationwide suicide prevention and support network.

Call 1-833-456-4566 24/7 · Free · Confidential

🇦🇺 Australia — Lifeline

Crisis support and suicide prevention services.

Call 13 11 14 24/7 · Free · Confidential

🇮🇳 India — Vandrevala Foundation

24/7 crisis helpline for anyone in emotional distress.

Call 9999 666 555 24/7 · Free · Confidential

🇮🇳 India — iCall (TISS)

Email and phone counseling by trained mental health professionals.

Call 022-2552 1111 Mon-Sat · 10am-8pm IST
